-Formatting:
-use \left( and \right) where necessary (adapts size of parentheses/brackets to whats inside)
-use ~ for space when referencing an equation, lemma, section, etc. 
Example: from Equation~(\ref{eq:some_equation})


-Style:
-when justifying a statement, prefer 'by this fact' or 'since equation' to 'because this fact' or 'because equation'
